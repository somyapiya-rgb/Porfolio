My Interests

Below are some topics I care about and hobbies I enjoy.

Business

I am interested in business because it is about how people create value and help others. I like learning how a small idea can become a product or a service, how prices and plans are chosen, and how companies listen to customers. Business also teaches patience, planning, and how to work with different kinds of people.

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship means starting something of your own and taking responsibility for it. I find it exciting when someone notices a problem and tries to solve it with a new idea. It involves creativity, risk-taking, and learning from mistakes while building something meaningful.

Being a software engineer

I want to become a software engineer in the future. Software engineers write code to build websites, apps, and tools that people use every day. I like the idea of turning a clear design into something that works on a screen and helps people save time or learn something new. I know it takes a lot of practice, but I am ready to keep learning programming, fix bugs calmly, and work with others on projects.

Sports hobbies

Football: I enjoy football as a hobby. Running on the field, passing the ball, and playing with a team helps me stay active and happy. Football also reminds me that we cannot win alone, and teamwork, trust, and effort from everyone are important.

Basketball: I also like basketball. It is fast and fun, and it improves my focus and coordination. Playing basketball with friends is a good break from study time, and it keeps my body strong and my mind fresh.
